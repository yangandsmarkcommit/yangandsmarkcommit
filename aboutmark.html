Biography

Mark spent his first 18 years growing up in Pacific Palisades, California. The fateful introduction of Mark to the “Weirdo Table” at Brentwood School helped him find his home playing RPGs and table-top miniature games. This same community of people founded KBWS, the high school radio station, which introduced Mark to Linux and computer programming. Mark then shipped out to MIT where he and Yang were freshmen together at the THiRD EAst Travelling Animal Zoo. They then parted ways for several years as Mark moved to tEp, the nerdy, netty, laser-light-showy fraternity that became his second home and family. Five years later, Yang and Mark reunited, but he was unable to reach escape velocity from MIT, landing at the wonderland warehouse space that was the Cruft Laboratories while he ground away at graduate school. Finally escaping with degree in hand, he waved goodbye to Boston, his home for the last decade and was reunited in Yang in the East Bay.
